Virelai

A Virelai is a Medieval poetry and musical form originating from France, similar to the ballad and rondeau. Few famous pieces are in Virelai form and as such it is often underrepresented when examining pieces from this time period.

Pitch Restrictions

I created this when I was experiencing a creative block on one of my other (still unpublished) pieces. I challenged myself to use five randomly generated pitches, four note lengths, and three instruments to create this piece.

One Little Duckling

This is my first formal composition piece, inspired by the ducklings that are born on campus every Spring. The piece tells the story of a baby duckling exploring the world while the mother duck watches over them.
